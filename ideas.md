<idea>
**Design Movement:** Dark Brutalist Tech — raw industrial grid meets precision data aesthetics
**Core Principles:**
- High contrast: near-black backgrounds with electric blue and white
- Monospaced data-terminal typography for labels, display serif for headlines
- Asymmetric grid — content bleeds to edges, columns break the expected rhythm
- Motion as data: animated counters, scanning lines, progress bars

**Color Philosophy:** #050A14 near-black base, #0057FF electric blue, #FFFFFF crisp white, #FF3B3B red for problem states. Feels like a mission control room — serious, precise, powerful.

**Signature Elements:**
- Thin horizontal rule lines with small labels (like engineering drawings)
- Data HUD overlays on images (ATTEMPTS: 240/500 style)
- Grid dot pattern as section backgrounds

**Animation:** Subtle scan-line on hero load, number counters animate in on scroll, section reveals with 20px upward fade.

<idea>
**Design Movement:** Cinematic Dark Sci-Fi — like a product launch for a space company or advanced robotics lab
**Core Principles:**
- Deep dark backgrounds with subtle blue-purple gradient atmosphere
- Photography treated as cinematic stills — large, dramatic, full-bleed

**Color Philosophy:** #060D1A deep space blue-black, #0057FF electric blue, #7C3AED violet accent for vision sections, #FFFFFF for key text. Emotional, ambitious, forward-looking.

**Layout Paradigm:** Full-viewport sections. Hero is cinematic with large centered headline and atmospheric background. Content sections alternate between text-heavy and visual-heavy.

**Signature Elements:**
- Glowing blue horizontal divider lines between sections
- Frosted glass cards for feature descriptions
- Subtle particle/dot grid in hero background

**Typography System:** DM Sans (800) for display, Inter (400/500) for body, Space Grotesk (700) for accent labels. Headlines 72–96px with tight letter-spacing.

**Animation:** Parallax hero, fade-in on scroll, subtle glow pulse on accent elements.

## Selected: Option 3 — Cinematic Dark Sci-Fi

Deep, atmospheric, ambitious. Matches the SIM XR brand: cutting-edge robotics + XR + AI. The dark palette with blue/violet accents communicates "frontier technology" without being cold or corporate. Photography of VR users and robots becomes cinematic rather than illustrative.
