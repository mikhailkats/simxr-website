Pour coffee from kettle to mug

●●● HARD HIGH PAY 5 episodes needed ~9 min each Bimanual · Liquid
TARGET BEHAVIOR PREVIEW

BRIEF

WHAT

Using both arms, lift the kettle from the countertop, bring it above the white mug, tilt to pour approximately 120ml of coffee, and return the kettle to its starting position. Complete 5 clean episodes.

WHY

This task trains fine bimanual liquid-handling policies for GR1T2's home-assistant stack. Clean episodes feed directly into this week's mid-training run at Fourier.

ACCEPTANCE
  • ✓ Liquid volume in mug between 100–140ml
  • ✓ No spill outside 5cm radius of mug
  • ✓ Kettle returned within 2cm of start pose
  • ✓ Episode duration 6–12 minutes

SUGGESTED FLOW

  1. 01
    Approach & grasp kettle
    Left hand on handle, right hand on body for stability. 0.5-0.8s grasp window.
  2. 02
    Lift & traverse to mug
    Keep kettle level. Clear the toaster by at least 8cm.
  3. 03
    Tilt & pour
    Approach the rim at 15°. Start pouring at 25° tilt, increase smoothly to ~55°. Hold until target volume.
  4. 04
    Retract & return
    Level the kettle before moving. Place within 2cm of start pose.
